Our Services

Chemical supplying and production of quality paint since 2009. Serving industrial, commercial, and residential clients across Nigeria.

Building (Decorative Paint)

We produce and supply high-quality decorative paints for both internal and external applications. Our comprehensive after-sales services ensure complete customer satisfaction and long-lasting results for residential and commercial projects.

Industrial Paint

Specialized painting services for industrial machinery and equipment. We also provide high-quality road marking paint for transportation infrastructure projects, ensuring durability and visibility in all weather conditions.
