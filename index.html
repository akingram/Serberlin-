Frequently Asked Questions

Find simple answers to common questions about our paints, colors, and services. If you need more help, just ask us.

Choosing colors is easy when you think about a few simple things:

  • Room size: Light colors make small rooms look bigger. Dark colors make big rooms feel cozy.
  • Sunlight: Does the room get lots of sun? Dark colors look good in sunny rooms.
  • Room use: Is it a bedroom, kitchen, or living room? We can suggest good colors for each.
  • Your taste: What colors do you like? We have many options.

Example: For a bedroom, you can try one dark wall and three light walls. It looks nice!

Come to our shop and we'll help you choose. We have color samples you can take home.

We have 5 main types of paint finishes:

  • Matte (Flat): No shine. Hides wall marks well. Good for ceilings and bedrooms.
  • Eggshell: Little shine. Easy to clean. Good for living rooms.
  • Satin: Soft shine. Easy to wipe clean. Best for kitchens and bathrooms.
  • Semi-Gloss: Shiny. Very strong. Good for doors and windows.
  • High-Gloss: Very shiny. Super strong. For furniture and cabinets.

Simple tip: More shine = easier to clean. Less shine = hides wall marks better.

Our paints are made to last long:

  • Inside paint: 5-10 years
  • Outside paint: 7-15 years

What makes paint last longer:

  • Clean walls before painting
  • Use our primer first
  • Paint 2 thin coats (not 1 thick coat)
  • Less sun = longer lasting paint

Our Serberlin premium paints last longer because we use quality materials.
