Like any other decision, when you want to decide the right colour palette for your home, you need to start with practical considerations. In this case, they are the size of the room, the amount of sunlight the room gets, what the room will be used for, and of course, your budget.

Next, you come to your paint colour preferences. Do you like dark paint colours for home or are you looking for the best light colour for home? Each one of the above details will decide the colour code you should go for.

For example, let's assume you want to select the right wall paint colour for your bedroom. The room is relatively big with a medium-sized window. In this case, you can explore the darker bedroom Berger paints colour shades for one wall combined with lighter colours for the other three.

We offer a wide range of paint finishes to suit different needs and preferences:

  • Matte/Flat: Non-reflective finish that hides surface imperfections well, ideal for ceilings and low-traffic areas.
  • Eggshell: Low-sheen finish with good durability, perfect for living rooms and bedrooms.
  • Satin: Soft sheen that's easy to clean, great for kitchens, bathrooms, and trim.
  • Semi-Gloss: Reflective and highly durable, excellent for high-moisture areas and trim work.
  • High-Gloss: Maximum shine and durability, perfect for doors, cabinets, and furniture.

Our premium paints are formulated for exceptional durability and longevity. When applied correctly to properly prepared surfaces, our interior paints typically last 5-10 years before needing a refresh, while our exterior paints can maintain their appearance and protective qualities for 7-15 years depending on environmental conditions.

Factors that affect paint longevity include:

  • Surface preparation
  • Application technique
  • Environmental exposure (sunlight, moisture, temperature fluctuations)
  • Quality of primer used
  • Number of coats applied

Yes, we offer professional custom color matching services. If you have a specific color in mind that isn't in our standard palette, bring us a sample (such as a fabric swatch, photo, or existing paint chip) and our experts can create a perfect match using our advanced color matching technology.

Coverage can vary depending on the surface texture, porosity, and application method, but as a general guideline:

  • Our interior paints typically cover 350-400 square feet per gallon for the first coat on properly prepared surfaces.
  • Our exterior paints generally cover 250-350 square feet per gallon, depending on surface texture.
  • Primers typically cover 200-300 square feet per gallon, as they're designed to penetrate and seal surfaces.

For best results, we recommend:

  • Proper surface preparation (cleaning, sanding, and priming when necessary)
  • Using high-quality brushes and rollers
  • Applying two thin coats rather than one thick coat
  • Following the specific product instructions for optimal coverage
